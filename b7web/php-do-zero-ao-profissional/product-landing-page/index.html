What is SmartLens?




Have you ever forgotten what to say during a speech in front of your audience?
Or have you ever gotten very nervous in front of your audience having a hard time to present?
Your problems end today.

What you will see today is not only a product.
It's a technological breakthorugh.
Without further ado, let's get started.

SmartLens is a product developed by VFM2.
Roughly speaking, it places a film on the lens of a glass and the placed film displays content.

Why should you use it?




First of all, I would like to state that the SmartLens has being tested thousands of times, so we assure you it's completely safe.

The SmartLens has as it main target people who
need a well-organized speech to execute their work,
just like professors, doctors and even students!

Our product also includes optional micro-speakers,
which can be paired to bluetooth earphones,
to help our customers provide their speeches easily.

Our stores cover most part of the globe, so in addition to
an advanced technology and a good price,
you will be able to buy it anywhere in the world!

Features




The SmartLens technology uses the most recent technology
available in the market to provide our clients,
the consumer, the best UX possible.

It uses a simple bluetooth connection to display - right in front of your eyes - whatever is
being shown on our proprietary app.

Which is available on nearly all mobile devices,
running Windows, Linux, Android and iOS operating systems.

Show presentations from some other apps,
such as, PowerPoint, Word, Pages, Google Slides, etc... on your computer
or even show a video from YouTube.

We care about your eyes!
Our lenses were built using eye-friendly technology,
which protect your eyes from UV and light-radiation emitted by ordinary screen pixels,
avoiding eye irritation and redness, even during prolonged use.

Includes a built-in special film which blocks other people
from seeing what is being shown on the lenses;
guaranteeing maximum convenience and security to the user.
